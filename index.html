Hi, I'm Amina.

It’s good to see you here. A few words about me. I’m an experienced content manager and photographer with a lot of passion for my job. I know exactly how to build a strong content strategy and apply it within the web site and social media platforms.

My journey of visual content creation started in 2010 when I successfully graduated from the School of Photo Art. I got into photography and started building my career in this field.

In a few years, I discovered an interesting world of digital marketing. I fell in love with how dynamic and fast-moving it is, you can always learn something new, gain your experience and skills. I started of as a copywriter, then I've learnt SEO optimization and started using my English language skills to extend the variaty of projects that I could work with.

Next step in my growth was social media. I had a lot to learn, but having quite good experience in both photography and SEO-copywriting let me run branded social media platforms full circle already. But it wasn't enough for me, so I strengthened my knowledge by learning Google Analytics and different analytical tools, such as Pulsar, Social Studio and Buffer.

If you ask me to list all the fields that I worked in, it would take me quite some time (not because I have a bad memory). I've created content for travel, accounting, construction, medical companies, beauty salons, food delivery services, taxi services, news and entertainment websites, etc. As you can see I'm familiar with the content specifics of different areas.

I'm always excited to try new things, obsessed with the filmmaking process, theatre, and photography.
